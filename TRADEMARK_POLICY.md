# Trademark Policy (Placeholder)

> **Status: pending JPO trademark registration.**

> "Cooperative Agent Protocol" and "CAP" (in the context of the Cooperative

This document will be finalized once the JPO registration completes
(expected 8-10 months after filing). Until then, the following provisional
rules apply:

## Provisional rules (pre-registration)

1. **Spec, code, documentation**: free to use, copy, modify, and distribute
   under the Apache License 2.0 (see [LICENSE](LICENSE)). Naming the project
   in references, citations, and dependencies is encouraged.

2. **"Conformant implementation" claims**: implementations may state
   compatibility with a specific CAP version (e.g., "compatible with
   CAP-Core v0.1") in factual, descriptive language. Marketing claims of
   "CAP Conformant" with associated badges/logos will be governed by a
   formal conformance certification process once the trademark is
   registered (see [ADR-011](docs/adr/011-domain-pack-architecture.md)).

3. **Forks and derivative projects**: must not use the unqualified name
   "Cooperative Agent Protocol" or "CAP" in a way that implies official
   endorsement. Suggested naming: "MyProject (built on CAP)" or
   "MyProject — a fork of the Cooperative Agent Protocol".

4. **Domain Pack names**: third-party Domain Packs must use the
   `cap.v0.extensions.<org>.<name>` namespace per ADR-011 and avoid the
   bare `cap.v0.domains.<name>` namespace reserved for officially
   approved Packs.
